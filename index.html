My Story

Growing up, I noticed that girls were often underrepresented in science and technology classes. This inspired me to become a voice for change. My journey in tech began with curiosity and a desire to break barriers. Today, as a Software Engineering student at ALU, I am dedicated to empowering women to pursue their dreams in STEM. Through community work, mentorship, and building innovative solutions, I strive to create opportunities for girls and women to shine in technology. I believe that when women are empowered, the world becomes a better, more innovative place for everyone.
